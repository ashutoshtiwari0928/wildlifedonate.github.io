About us

Our mission

The goal of wildlife donation is to have a major and long-lasting positive effect on the preservation and protection of various ecosystems, threatened species, and the planet's overall biodiversity.

Our vision

Coexisting in the same landscape in a manner in which both conservation and welfare are addressed for man and wildlife.

Our history

Established in 1940, Madumalai National Park has a rich conservation history, with endangered species conservation and research, diverse wildlife and lush landscapes that attract visitors.

These are just some of those who need your help!

LEO

Meet Leo, a courageous and seasoned lion who has led an adventurous life. Leo needs specialized care and attention in his senior years to ensure his wellbeing. Leo can benefit greatly from your gift by receiving the veterinary care, medication, and cozy surroundings he requires to live out his golden years in dignity.

Ella

Elephant Ella, who is kind and strong, has recovered from a serious wound. Ella's unwavering spirit continues to motivate us all in spite of her disability. By making a donation, you can aid Ella's ongoing recovery and help the committed team of caregivers who offer her basic medical care, enrichment activities, and a secure environment where she can live her life to the fullest.

Tara

Beautiful tiger Tara recently fought a crippling illness. Tara is now on the road to recovery thanks to a life-saving medical procedure. Tara can be given the ongoing care she requires, such as specialized diets, frequent checkups, and a comfortable environment, thanks to your kind donation. You are preserving this threatened species and giving tigers all over the world hope by contributing to Tara's journey.
